Privacy Bridge

Cross-chain private transfers between Flow EVM and Starknet. Deposit on one chain, withdraw on another. No link between them.

01
Deposit
Send a fixed denomination into the source pool. Your commitment is hashed and inserted into an on-chain Merkle tree. No link to your identity leaves the transaction.
Flow EVM
02
Prove
Generate a Groth16 zero-knowledge proof locally. The proof demonstrates you know a valid secret and nullifier for an existing commitment, without revealing which one.
Client-side
03
Relay
A relayer submits your proof to the destination chain. You never interact with the destination directly, so your withdrawal address stays unlinkable.
Relayer Network
04
Withdraw
Funds arrive at your destination address after the timelock clears. The nullifier is marked spent. The same commitment can never be used twice.
Starknet
  • Fixed denomination pools
    Eliminates amount-based linking between deposits and withdrawals
  • On-chain Merkle tree
    Poseidon incremental, depth 24, trustlessly verifiable by anyone
  • Commitment hiding
    Poseidon(Poseidon(secret, nullifier), amount) -- double-hash prevents pre-image extraction
  • No privacy-leaking events
    Mint emits only nullifier hash and amount, never the commitment pre-image
  • Relayer support
    Anyone can submit proofs on your behalf, so users never reveal their address
  • Withdrawal timelock
    Configurable delay prevents timing correlation between deposit and withdrawal
  • Emergency withdraw
    30-day timelock, owner-initiated, cancellable -- funds are never permanently locked
