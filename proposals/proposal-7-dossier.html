── Protocol Flow ── Unclassified ──
1
Lock
Deposit a fixed denomination into the source chain bridge contract. A Poseidon commitment is inserted into the on-chain Merkle tree.
chain: Flow EVM
2
Prove
Generate a Groth16 proof of Merkle membership without revealing the deposit leaf. The proof includes nullifier hash and recipient address.
chain: Off-chain
3
Verify
Garaga verifier checks the Groth16 proof on Starknet. Nullifier is recorded to prevent double-spend.
chain: Starknet
4
Claim
Tokens minted to recipient address on Starknet. The Mint event emits only nullifier_hash and amount. No link to source deposit.
chain: Starknet
